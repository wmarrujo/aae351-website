Executive Summary

Our project aims to provide an initial analysis for a concept process of the removal of unexploded ordnance in weapons ranges. This analysis is focused on the risk and practicality of the missions and our proposed approaches due to the sensitive nature of the requirements. We have proposed a plausible solution based on existing, mature technology that can be easily integrated. Our solution involves working on the contaminated areas in piecemeal, 100 acre segments, with multiple robots completing the defusal, disposal or detonation of munitions under the supervision of experienced UXO (unexploded ordnance) disposal technicians. Our solution primarily aims to minimize risk of bodily harm and a harmful lasting impact on the environment.
