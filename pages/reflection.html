Reflection

Lessons Learnt

A few key lessons we learnt were:

  • Price is the driving factor in many decisions
  • It is easier to design around existing technologies during concept generation
  • Certain critical requirements such as casualties cannot be compromised during risk assessment.
  • Not all designs have patentable aspects.
  • Multiple ideas can be merged to form a single final concept.

Next Steps

The next steps would be tactical planning on the specific implementation of each search & disposal procedure for each of the specific ranges and the designing and manufacture of the robots used in our solution. A more in-depth analysis of the specific terrain and types of ordinance at each will undoubtedly change the specifics of our design.
